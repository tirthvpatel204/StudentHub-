Support FAQ Center

Browse our catalog of frequently asked questions regarding the StudentHub intranet workspace.

General Questions

What is StudentHub?

StudentHub is a consolidated college student portal designed as a semester project. It integrates registrations, syllabus databases, schedule sheets, and support modules into a single accessible workspace.

How do I obtain support if I encounter a portal bug?

You can file an official helpdesk ticket on the Contact Us page. Choose the "Technical glitch" subject, outline the issue, and provide your university registration ID.

Academic & Course Regulations

How is semester attendance computed on the dashboard?

Attendance is computed based on daily RFID check-ins at laboratory entries and lecturer rolls. Class records update every 24 hours onto the active Student Dashboard metrics.

Where do I find details about elective courses syllabus?

Elective syllabus copies and prerequisite qualifications can be obtained by reading the portal guides on the About Page or checking corresponding department files in the library.

Technical Support & Security

How do I change my registered password?

Access your settings on the Student Profile, navigate to the password section, enter your current institutional password, and specify a new secure character combination.

What should I do if my registration application is pending review?

New profile registrations go through dean office moderation. Verification is usually completed in 2 days. If it exceeds 3 days, please mail your records to the registrar office.
